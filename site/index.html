About this mirror

GitHub stopped publishing aggregate uptime numbers — you can probably guess why. This is the missing version.

We rebuild platform‑wide and per‑service uptime from archived status updates, and we surface the incidents that drive those numbers. Everything is open source, and PRs are very welcome.
